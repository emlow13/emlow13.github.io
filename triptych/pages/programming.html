Initial Setup

Triptych is happy to create custom programming for your CrossFit affiliate or other gym on a monthly basis. However, before your custom programming begins, you need to be able to answer most of the questions below. We can help with filling in the blanks if necessary.

  • How many times does your average client work out daily?
  • What is your main focus area as a gym (i.e. endurance, gymnastics, or weighlighting?)
  • What are your clients currently most frustrated with in their progress?
