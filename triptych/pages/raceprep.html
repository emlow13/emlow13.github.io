5K/10K Race Prep

If you're planning to run a 5K or 10K in a few months and aren't sure what to do next, Triptych is here to help. Want to get faster? Want to train while recovering from an injury? Want to run as little as possible? Our training plans meet those needs.

Training Plans

5K Run

For a customized training plan, click the link below with the responses to the following questions. Prior to receiving the training plan, you must take a short video of yourself to ensure your commitment and understand your current running form:

  • What is your best mile time within the last year? If you don't know it, what is the fastest treadmill setting you're comfortable with?
  • How far away is your race day?
  • What is your goal for this race? (Have fun, run faster, prevent injury, etc.)?
